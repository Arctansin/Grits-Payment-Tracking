## 1. Who We Are

Grits Payment Tracker is an independent, personally developed application created to help individuals track their own debt accounts (including credit cards and loans), balances, and due dates. We are not a bank, financial institution, or licensed financial advisor.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2a. Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use the App, you may provide:

- **Account credentials** — your email address and password, used for registration and login via Firebase Authentication
- **Credit card tracking data** — card name, last four digits of card number, current balance, due date, last payment date, minimum payment amount, APR, promotional APR, and promotional period length
- **AI chat content** — messages, questions, images, or documents (such as credit card statements) that you submit to the AI assistant feature

We do not collect your full credit card numbers, CVV codes, or Social Security Number.

### 2b. Information Collected via Third-Party Services

If you choose to connect a bank account using the Plaid integration, Plaid will collect and process your banking credentials and account information directly. **We do not receive, store, or have access to your bank login credentials or full account numbers.** Plaid provides us only with the account balance and account name you authorize for display in the App.

### 2c. Technical Information

The App may process basic operational metadata required by our service providers to deliver authentication, cloud sync, and AI/Plaid requests. We do not intentionally collect analytics profiles or sell behavioral data.

## 3. How We Store Your Data

The App offers two storage modes, which you can control in Settings:

### Cloud Sync Enabled (Default)

Your credit card tracking data is stored in Google Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google Cloud Platform. Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest per Google's security standards. Your data is associated with your account and accessible across devices when you are signed in.

### Cloud Sync Disabled (Local Only)

When you opt out of cloud sync, all data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. In this mode:

- We have zero access to your data
- Your data is not transmitted to any server
- **If your device is lost, stolen, damaged, or reset, your data cannot be recovered.** There is no backup.

You may switch between modes at any time in the App's Settings.

## 5. AI Features and Data Visibility

The App includes an optional AI assistant that can read credit card statements and answer questions about your finances.

### Using Your Own OpenAI API Key

If you provide your own OpenAI API key in Settings, your messages and any files or images you upload are sent directly from your device to OpenAI's servers under your own OpenAI account. We do not see, receive, or store any of this content.

### Using Our Hosted API Key

If you use the AI feature without providing your own key, your requests are routed through our backend, which forwards them to OpenAI. In this configuration:

- The content of your messages and any uploaded images or documents **are technically visible to the app developer**
- We do not actively monitor, log for personal use, sell, or share this content
- Your data is still transmitted to and processed by OpenAI and subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy
- **We strongly recommend using your own OpenAI API key if you prefer that no one other than OpenAI can access your AI interactions**

You can set your own API key at any time in the App's Settings.
